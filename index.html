About Me

I am an Academic Administration and Admissions Specialist with over 12 years of experience at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK Surathkal), an Institute of National Importance under the Government of India.

My expertise spans the complete student lifecycle — from application and document verification, eligibility assessment, enrolment confirmation, through to graduation and degree certification — across 40+ UG, PG and PhD programmes.

I bring demonstrated proficiency in Oracle database management, Academic ERP (IRIS), centralized admission portals (JoSAA, CCMT, CCMN), and compliance reporting to MoE, UGC, NBA, NIRF and AISHE. I am an IBM Database Certified professional and MCA postgraduate.

As Lead DASA & CIWG Verification Officer, I have managed international admissions for Gulf country students (Kuwait, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman) with 100% verification accuracy across 15,000+ applications.
